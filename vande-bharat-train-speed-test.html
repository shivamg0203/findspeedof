What is Vande Bharat Express?

Vande Bharat Express formerly known as Train 18 is India's flagship semi-high-speed, fully air-conditioned passenger train connecting cities which are less than 800 km apart or take less than ten hours to travel. It is designed under the Make in India initiative, featuring a sleek aerodynamic design, automatic doors, comfortable seating, and advanced onboard facilities.

Vande Bharat train offers a premium travel experience, it represents a major step forward in India's railway modernization, providing smoother rides, improved safety, and world-class amenities for passengers across major intercity routes.
