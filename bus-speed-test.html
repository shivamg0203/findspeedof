What is Bus Speed Tester?

A Bus Speed Tester is an online GPS-based tool that allows passengers to measure the real-time traveling speed of a bus directly from their device.

City buses typically operate at speeds ranging from 20 km/h to 60 km/h, depending on traffic conditions, road quality, signals, and local regulations. Highway buses may reach higher speeds.

This tool is useful for passengers who want to estimate travel speed during daily commuting or long-distance journeys. Speed accuracy may vary based on GPS signal strength and surroundings.
